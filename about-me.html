I’m a 22 years old student and I started studying design 8 years ago. At first I was mainly interested in animation and motion design, so I learned much about them during my high school studies. However when my application was accepted to the university I was introduced to web design and frontend which dug deep into my hearth.

Over the past few years I learned a lot about different softwares and different design languages and rules. I learned about most of the Adobe programs, how to make a working typeface, how to design a brand, how to cut videos, how to make 3D renders and so on. While I was working on projects for school lessons, clients or just because I wanted to chill out I have got the chance to develop myself and meeting other people. Anyways I hope you are having fun on my portfolio site. Wish you a good day!
