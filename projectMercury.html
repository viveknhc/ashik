Mercury was my second web design project, where I learned much about the design and the frontend process. Its a conceptual website, where people can buy water from other planets and moons in our solar system. Ironically for the company's name Mercury, which is a poisonous and undrinkable substance they actualy provide some really good water. The website is currently only a prototype, where I usde code libraries like: JQuery, GSAP, and THREE.js
